Zhuofei Lu

I am a postdoctoral researcher in Sociology and course covenor in Global Area Study at Oxford, working for the ‘GenTime’ project. I have consistently engaged in research projects related to gig economy, flexible working, occupational well-being, time use, and social inequality in the labour market. I serve the editoral board of Work Employment & Society & Social Science Research.

I employ computational and other quantitative methods (e.g., sequence analysis, causal inference and machine learning) for analysing large-scale longitudinal household panel data, time diary data and biomarker data.

I am from China, Guangdong, Shenzhen/Chaozhou.
